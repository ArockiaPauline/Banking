Privacy Policy

CrownBank is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers' personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose personal information in connection with our banking services.

1. Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information from our customers to provide banking services and to comply with legal and regulatory requirements. The types of personal information we collect may include:

  • Name, address, and other contact information
  • Date of birth and social security number
  • Financial information, such as income, assets, and liabilities
  • Transaction information, such as account balances, payment history, and credit card usage
  • Employment information, such as job title and employer name

We may collect personal information directly from our customers, from third-party sources such as credit reporting agencies, and from publicly available sources.

2. Use of Personal Information

We use personal information to provide banking services to our customers, to manage our business operations, and to comply with legal and regulatory requirements. Examples of how we use personal information include:

  • Processing transactions and account maintenance
  • Assessing creditworthiness and making lending decisions
  • Detecting and preventing fraud and other illegal activities
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements, such as reporting to government agencies and responding to court orders and legal investigations
  • Communicating with our customers about their accounts and banking services

3. Disclosure of Personal Information

We may disclose personal information to third parties in connection with our banking services and as required or permitted by law. Examples of third parties we may disclose personal information to include:

  • Service providers, such as data processors and payment processors
  • Government agencies, such as tax authorities and law enforcement
  • Credit reporting agencies and other financial institutions
  • Legal and financial advisors

We require third parties to maintain appropriate safeguards to protect personal information and to use personal information only for the purposes for which it was disclosed.

4. Protection of Personal Information

We maintain appropriate technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We limit access to personal information to those employees, contractors, and agents who need to know the information to perform their job duties.

5. Your Rights

You have certain rights with respect to your personal information, including the right to access and correct your personal information, the right to request that we delete your personal information, and the right to object to the processing of your personal information. Please contact us using the information below to exercise your rights.

6. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify our customers of any material changes to this privacy policy by posting a notice on our website or by other means as required by law.
