Who Can Apply?

Customer Eligibility

  • All resident Indians (Salaried, Self-Employed, House wife, Students, etc.) aged between 18 and 70 years are eligible for our Gold Loans.

Accepted collateral:

  • Gold jewellery of purity between 18 to 22 karats
  • Bank-issued gold coins up to 50 grams per customer
  • Gold idols/bars/watches/utensils/other coins/ white gold are not acceptable

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is a Gold Loan?
CROWN Gold Loan is a loan against gold jewellery (within a range of 18-22 karats) taken by the borrower as a primary security. You can also apply online for a loan against gold without submitting much documentation.
Why should I apply for Gold Loans from crown Bank?
As a pioneer in paperless and digital lending, crown Bank has extended its cutting-edge technology in digital lending to Gold Loans, allowing instant credit of funds using biometrics
What are the available schemes for Gold Loan in crown Bank at present?
i. Bullet Scheme: The interest and principal amount are payable at the end of the Gold Loan tenure ii. Monthly Interest Scheme: The interest amount is paid monthly and the principal is repaid at the end of the tenure iii. Equated Monthly Instalments (EMI): Under the EMI scheme, a part of the principal and interest is paid at regular monthly intervals
