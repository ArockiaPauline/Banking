FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is a Personal Loan?
Crown Bank personal loans help you borrow money for expenses during financial emergencies, for going on a vacation, or for other personal expenses such as a wedding in the family. You can avail a personal loan at minimum interest rates for amounts ranging from ₹20,000 to ₹1 crore and for tenures ranging between six months and five years.
Why should I take a lowest rate interest Personal Loan?
You can apply online for personal loans from crown Bank for a wide variety of reasons such as weddings, holidays, education, home renovation, and for emergencies. Personal Loans can help address your immediate needs and can also be used for borrowing money for not-so-urgent personal expenses.
Do I need to provide any security or collateral to apply for an instant Personal Loan?
Instant Personal Loans are not secured by any collateral or security and are disbursed at the discretion of crown Bank, based on the creditworthiness of the borrower.
