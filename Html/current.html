FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Current Account?
A current account is a type of bank account that allows frequent and unlimited deposits and withdrawals.
How can I apply for a current account?
You can visit the nearest bank branch with all the required documents and apply it.
What is the overdraft facility in a current account?
An overdraft facility is a credit limit that is provided by the bank in a current account. It allows the account holder to withdraw more money than the available balance in the account, up to a certain limit.
What is the difference between a savings account and a current account?
A savings account is designed for individuals who want to save money and earn interest on their savings, while a current account is designed for businesses and individuals who need to make frequent and large transactions.
