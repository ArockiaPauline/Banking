FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Savings Account?
A savings account is a type of bank account that is designed to help individuals save money and earn interest on their savings. It is typically used for short-term savings goals, such as building an emergency fund, saving for a down payment on a home, or setting aside money for a vacation.
What is the minimum balance required to open a savings account?
The minimum balance required to open a savings account varies by bank and account type. Here, we don't have minimum balance requirements.
Can I access my savings account online?
Yes, Crown Bank offer online banking services that allow you to access your savings account and perform transactions, such as checking your account balance, transferring funds, and bill payments.
