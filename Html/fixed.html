Features

An FD investment is valuable in many ways such as

  • High-Interest Rates: Fixed deposits offer higher interest rates compared to savings accounts, allowing customers to earn more on their savings.
  • Fixed Tenure: Fixed deposits have a fixed tenure, ranging from a few months to several years. Customers can choose the tenure that suits their needs.
  • Low-Risk Investment: Fixed deposits are a low-risk investment option as they offer guaranteed returns, making it a secure investment for customers.
  • Flexible Investment Amounts: Customers can invest in fixed deposits with a minimum investment amount, making it accessible to a wide range of customers.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is a fixed deposit?
A fixed deposit is a financial instrument where customers can invest a lump sum amount for a fixed period at a predetermined interest rate.
What is the minimum and maximum tenure of a fixed deposit?
The minimum and maximum tenure of a fixed deposit may vary based on the bank's policies and regulations. Generally, the minimum tenure is 7 days, while the maximum tenure can go up to 10 years or more.
Can I withdraw my fixed deposit before maturity?
Yes, customers can withdraw their fixed deposit before maturity. However, banks may charge a penalty fee for premature withdrawal.
Is the interest on fixed deposit taxable?
Yes, the interest earned on fixed deposits is taxable. The tax liability depends on the individual's income tax bracket.
